📊 Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

Project Overview

This project is an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard developed in Microsoft Power BI to provide business stakeholders with valuable insights into sales performance, profitability, customer demographics, and product category trends.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visualizations that enable decision-makers to monitor key business metrics, identify top-performing markets, and evaluate product profitability.

---

Business Problem

Businesses often collect large volumes of sales data but struggle to convert that information into actionable insights.

Without a centralized dashboard, it becomes difficult to answer important business questions such as:

- Which countries generate the most revenue?
- Which product categories are the most profitable?
- How does revenue differ between male and female customers?
- What is the overall profit margin?
- Which markets require strategic attention?

---

Project Objective

The objective of this project was to design a Power BI dashboard that enables management to:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Compare profitability across countries
- Analyse customer demographics
- Evaluate product category performance
- Support strategic business decisions through data visualization

---

Dashboard KPIs

The dashboard includes the following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Cost
- Average Unit Cost
- Average Unit Price
- Average Profit Margin


Dashboard Visualizations

- Revenue by Customer Gender
- Profit Margin by Product Category
- Profit Margin by Country
- Cost vs Profit by Country
- Executive KPI Cards
